BOXING

"I’ve been throwing punches and learning the art of self-defense for a solid 10 years now, covering everything from Krav Maga to Taekwondo, Jiu-Jitsu, Muay Thai, and boxing. Combat sports are my thing whether I’m training, watching fights ringside, or cheering from the couch.
